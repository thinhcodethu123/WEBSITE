Our Mission

Fan repair engineers are responsible for inspecting, repairing, maintaining and testing fans to ensure proper operation. They also give advice on how to use and maintain the fan to avoid errors.

Good Skill Engineers

Fan repair engineers have a variety of skills to ensure a quiet, efficient and safe fan operation. Some of the required skills include knowledge of mechatronics and electrical equipment, the ability to read technical drawings, skills in the use of measuring tools and equipment, and the ability to analyze and solve problems. , and communication and teamwork skills. In addition, time management skills and creative brainstorming to find optimal solutions are also very important.

Foreign engineers team

The cooperation between a team of foreign and Vietnamese engineers can bring many benefits to both sides. First, a team of foreign engineers can bring knowledge and experience in the field of fan repair, helping to strengthen the capacity and quality of the engineering team in Vietnam. In addition, this cooperation can also bring opportunities for Vietnamese enterprises in the field of fan manufacturing and trading to learn and develop.
